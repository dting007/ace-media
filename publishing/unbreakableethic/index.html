The Author
Daniel
Ting

Daniel Ting has spent his career doing what most people only plan to do — moving. Across industries, disciplines, and roles spanning live events, hospitality, social work, and education, he has consistently found a way to open doors that others assumed were closed, not through connections or credentials, but through the kind of deliberate, daily effort that this book is built on.

As a live events professional turned development coach and motivational speaker, Daniel has worked alongside hundreds of people at every level — and noticed a pattern that wouldn't leave him alone. The people furthest from the life they wanted weren't the ones without opportunity. They were the ones waiting for it to arrive on its own.

Unbreakable Ethic is his answer to that pattern. Not a lecture. Not a theory. A genuine, practical tool — written for the person who is ready to stop waiting and start building.
